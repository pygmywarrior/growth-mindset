Hi!
I'm Nick and I want to help you to grow :)

I am a professor of psychology and social behavior at Stanford University and I challenge myself to grow and to make mistakes. But it wasn't always this way.

Back at high school I was afraid not to disappoint my parents. They had high expectations of me. The pressure was unbearable and I couldn't be truly myself because I was hiding behind the mask of intelligence and talent. When went to college to become laywer, as my parents wanted me to, I had to stop it somehow.

That's when realized one thing that completely changed my life. I developed a new attitude towards goals and failures. I called this idea Growth Mindset in opposition to Fixed Mindset I had before.

And that's what this book about. It's designed to help you adopt new mindset and become a fulfilled human being that grows every day and see everywhere occasion to learn.
